# spLeakage: Detect and Quantify Spatial Information Leakage

Diagnostic tools for spatial information leakage in predictive
modelling. `spLeakage` audits a train/test split or modelling workflow,
quantifies the leakage and the optimism it induces, and recommends an
appropriate validation strategy for the user's sampling design and
prediction target.

## Details

See `docs/VISION.md` for the methodology and roadmap. The package is in
early development; no functions are exported yet (phase P0).
